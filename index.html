HOW IT WORKS

From conversation to action in minutes

We built MeetSmart to integrate seamlessly into your workflow so you can focus on the discussion, not the notes.

1

Record & Transcribe

MeetSmart joins your meeting automatically, recording high-quality audio and providing real-time text transcription.

2

AI Analysis

Our advanced AI processes the conversation to extract the core summary, highlight key decisions, and detect action items.

3

Structured Delivery

After the meeting ends, everyone instantly receives a structured report with the summary, decisions, and assigned tasks.

Never forget an important decision

Many companies waste time because discussions are long and tasks are not clearly assigned. MeetSmart works quietly in the background to capture everything that matters.

  • Automatically highlights key decisions from the transcript
  • Detects action items and deadlines from the conversation
  • Creates automatic task lists for each team member instantly
